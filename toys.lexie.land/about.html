About VisunovOS

VisunovOS© is a narrative game engine developed by CupSoft Interactive®. Our goal is to bring interactive storycraft to the web and its netizens.

VOS is at its core a toolbox; something for an artist to use and add to so it fits their wants and needs. In future versions this will be made more explicit, with the added feature to add and modify tools within VOS itself.

FAQ

What is VOS currently capable of?
This current version of VisunovOS (v0.00.kj.12.1B9) is very incomplete, you can't even save your works or overall progress yet!
We have been adding a variety tools and toys to it however, to learn and see where limits and challenges lie. After today's Try Outs we will start with the bones that'll make up the 1.0 version of VisunovOS.
Current tools include:

  • DivBRUSH - A drawing toy that lets you draw with divs, including the many different CSS stylings that can be applied to them!
  • Recurse^3 - A binary tree fractal creator. Each branch is a div, and can be styled as such!
  • Pop-Up Collager - A toy to make collages out of pop-ups, with options to either display a blank page with injected HTML or a webpage of your choosing!
  • Poppy - The mascot of VisunovOS and your personal assistant. Albeit limited, her role in learning and playing with VisunovOS will grow with time!

Why is VOS modelled after Windows98?
Determining the form VisunovOS took a while, what should a web-bound game engine look like? A part of that process was reconstructing a faux-operating system in a web context, for vague and undiscernible reasons.
This turned out to be a very interesting form for the engine to assume, in how it both tempers and sets expectations (openable programs, a desktop to save to, closing, moving, and resizing windows).

How was VOS made?
VisunovOS in its current form is hand-written vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS. In the future a framework such as Svelte might be involved to clean up and smoothen out development. We want to embed our love for hand-crafted work in the tool itself!
